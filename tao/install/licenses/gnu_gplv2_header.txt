TAO is the leading open-source Computer-Based Assessment platform distributed under a GPLv2 non-upgradable license. TAO consists of a number of components that are either the sole property of Open Assessment Technologies S.A., or the Public Research Centre Henri Tudor, or joined property between Tudor, the University of Luxembourg and/or DIPF. TAO also integrates community member contributions and third-party open-source components released under compatible licenses and that remain the property of their respective owners.
